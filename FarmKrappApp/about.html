Welcome to the Farm Crap App

An app designed to help farmers make the most of your manure (slurry, FYM and poultry litter).

The app contains 3 components:

  • The calculator

  • The image library (to which you can add your own photos)

  • The record sheets

The calculator will determine the amount of crop-available key nutrients (N, P & K) within the manure at different spreading rates helping you decide how much to spread in order to meet the crop requirements, and also what this looks like.

The image library can be used as a visual reference guide to estimate the spreading rate of manure already applied to the field and therefore calculate the amount of crop available nutrients that have been applied.

The Farm Crap App can also be used to keep records of spreading events per field, how much manure was spread and what this translates to in terms of applied nutrients.

You can also upload a photo of the spreading event. The records can be emailed to you to be stored on your computer.

The data for the Farm Crap App has been compiled from the Defra RB209 Fertiliser Manual and the Think Manures handbook.
