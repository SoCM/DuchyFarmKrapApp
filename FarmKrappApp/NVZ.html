Farming in a Nitrate Vulnerable Zone

If you farm land within an NVZ (Nitrate Vulnerable Zone) there are legal restrictions on when you can apply slurries and manures to the land and how much you can apply in a calendar year.

To find the full list of compliance regulations please visit:

http://www.gov.uk/nitrate-vulnerable-zones

It is not permitted to spread organic manures to any field at a rate that would result in the TOTAL N supplied exceed 250kg N/ha in any rolling 12 month period.

The Farm Crap App does not calculate total N supply but rather crop available N. If you farm land in an NVZ you must make sure you are fully aware of the compliance rules, including closed periods for applying organic manures and total nitrogen supply.
